The Marguerite House

Where your wedding weekend truly begins.
Est. 1770s · Saint Gabriel, Louisiana

More Than a Venue Stay.
A Place to Gather.

The Marguerite House gives your wedding more room to breathe. You can arrive early, settle in with family, and spend the hours before the ceremony in a place that feels calm, private, and beautifully lived in. It is part getting-ready space, part overnight stay, and part memory-maker before the aisle ever begins.

This is not a generic hotel block or an off-site rental. The home is reserved for Chatsworth couples and their guests, which means the people around you are your people — and the entire weekend stays rooted on the estate.

The Experience

Arrive Friday.
Stay Present Through Sunday.

Friday evening, your family arrives one by one. Luggage rolls across old floors, someone opens a bottle of champagne, and the porch becomes the place everyone gathers while the light fades across the oaks.

Saturday morning, you wake up steps from where you will say your vows. No travel time. No pressure. Just a slower start, a better rhythm, and the feeling that the day belongs to you.

And because checkout is not until Sunday at noon, the final morning still feels like part of the celebration.
