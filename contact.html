The Process

Here’s What Happens
After You Reach Out

01

We Connect

Within 1–2 business days, Connie or a member of the team reaches out personally to learn about your vision, your guest count, and what matters most to you. Every couple is different, and the Chatsworth team genuinely takes the time to understand your day before anything else.

02

Walk the Grounds

We invite you out for a private walkthrough of the grounds — the gardens, the pavilions, the Marguerite House, every ceremony space. Most couples fall in love the moment they arrive. Seeing Chatsworth in person makes all the difference.

03

Reserve Your Date

When you are ready, a signed agreement and a $1,000 deposit secures your date exclusively. From that moment forward, the Chatsworth team is entirely devoted to making your day everything you have dreamed of.

04

Plan Your Day

Your dedicated coordinator walks with you through every detail — catering, ceremony setup, vendor coordination, and your day-of timeline, to the moment you say “I do.”
