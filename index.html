More Than a Venue.
A Full Weekend Experience.

Located in Saint Gabriel, just minutes from Baton Rouge, Chatsworth Plantation is a historic Louisiana wedding venue where couples can gather, stay on the estate, and celebrate without rushing through the day. Ancient oaks, water gardens, pavilions, and the private Marguerite House create a setting that feels relaxed, editorial, and deeply personal.

Chatsworth Plantation is a premier wedding venue in Saint Gabriel, Louisiana, offering a full weekend wedding experience with on-site accommodations, indoor and outdoor ceremony options, and private estate grounds just outside Baton Rouge.
