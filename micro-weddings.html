Our Philosophy

A Smaller Guest List.
The Same Sense of Occasion.

Some celebrations feel better when they stay intentionally small. With fewer guests, every moment lands differently — the vows feel closer, the dinner feels warmer, and the estate itself becomes part of the atmosphere instead of just the backdrop.

The grounds never feel oversized for a smaller event. They feel more personal. And if you want the weekend to stretch a little longer, the Marguerite House can turn an intimate celebration into a full estate stay.

What to Expect

What Your Day
Could Look Like

I

Morning at the Marguerite House

The estate is yours as early as 7:00 AM. No hotel. No drive. No rushing. Your wedding party gathers on the grounds, the morning unfolds completely on your schedule, and the day begins exactly the way it should — peacefully, beautifully, and on your terms.

II

The Ceremony

In your chosen ceremony space — beneath the ancient oaks, inside the Celeste Pavilion, or in the peaceful rose garden — you stand before the people who matter most. The beauty of these grounds has a way of making every word feel weightier, every moment more permanent.

III

Portraits on the Estate

Chatsworth was made for portraits. Towering oaks, tranquil water gardens, vine-draped pavilions, and the soft golden light of the Louisiana afternoon — your photographer will have everything they could dream of.

IV

The Celebration

In the Chatsworth Gallery or under the Brass Chandelier Pavilion, your guests gather for an intimate reception — catering by our in-house team, heartfelt toasts, and an evening everyone will remember.
