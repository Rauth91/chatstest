Welcome to Louisiana

Your Guests Will Love
It Here

Chatsworth Plantation is located at 5755 LA-74 in Saint Gabriel, Louisiana — just 20 minutes from Baton Rouge and under 90 minutes from New Orleans. The surrounding area is rich with Louisiana culture, cuisine, and history. Share this guide with your guests so they can make the most of their time in one of the most beautiful parts of the South.

For Your Out-of-Town Guests

Things to Do
While You're Here

Louisiana is unlike anywhere else in America. Whether your guests arrive a day early or stretch the weekend, they will find no shortage of beauty, history, and flavor waiting for them.

I
Plantation Tours
Oak Alley, Houmas House, and Nottoway Plantation are all within 30–45 minutes — stunning antebellum estates along the River Road.
II
New Orleans
Just 85 minutes away — the French Quarter, world-class dining, live jazz, and one of America's most unique cities. A perfect day trip.
III
Swamp & Bayou Tours
Experience the Atchafalaya Basin — the largest river swamp in the United States. Boat tours depart from nearby Henderson and Breaux Bridge.
IV
LSU Campus & Tiger Stadium
One of the most beautiful college campuses in the South. Stroll the live oak canopy, visit the museum, and soak in the gameday atmosphere.
V
Baton Rouge Food Scene
From boiled crawfish to Michelin-caliber Creole cuisine — Louisiana's capital has a food culture that rivals any city in America.
VI
Tanger Outlets & Shopping
Just minutes from the venue in Gonzales — perfect for last-minute wedding shopping or a leisurely afternoon for guests between events.
VII
Pelican Point Golf
Championship golf course just 20 minutes away — a great option for the groom's party the morning of the wedding or the day before.
VIII
Mississippi River
The estate sits in the Mississippi River corridor — one of the most historically rich and beautiful stretches of landscape in the entire South.
Getting Here

Getting to
Chatsworth

I.
By Air
Baton Rouge Metropolitan Airport (BTR) is the closest airport, approximately 30 minutes away. Louis Armstrong New Orleans International (MSY) is 85 minutes — with more flight options and often lower fares.
II.
By Car
From Baton Rouge take I-10 East to LA-74 South. From New Orleans take I-10 West. GPS address: 5755 LA-74, Saint Gabriel, LA 70776. Ample free parking on-site.
III.
Shuttles
We highly recommend arranging a shuttle from your hotel block to the venue. This allows guests to fully enjoy the evening. Ask us for local transportation vendor recommendations.
