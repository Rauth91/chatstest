Privacy Policy

Chatsworth Plantation · Saint Gabriel, Louisiana

This Privacy Policy explains how Chatsworth Plantation collects, uses, and protects your personal information, including how we handle your phone number if you choose to receive SMS text messages from us.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our contact form or otherwise communicate with us, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided and SMS consent is given)
  • Wedding date, guest count, and event details you share with us
  • Any other information you voluntarily include in your message

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your inquiry about booking or touring Chatsworth Plantation
  • Communicating with you about your event, appointment, or reservation
  • Sending you updates, reminders, or follow-up information directly related to your inquiry
  • Sending SMS text messages, if you have opted in to receive them

We will never use your information for unrelated marketing, and we will never sell, rent, lease, or share your personal information — including your phone number — with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

3. SMS Text Messaging — Opt-In & Terms

How We Use SMS

If you opt in to receive text messages from Chatsworth Plantation, we may contact you via SMS to follow up on your inquiry, confirm appointment or tour details, share information about your event, or respond to questions you have sent us. Our SMS communications are transactional and conversational in nature — directly related to your inquiry and potential event at our venue.

Opt-In Consent

SMS consent is entirely voluntary. You may opt in by checking the SMS consent box on our contact form. Submitting the form without checking that box will not result in text messages being sent to you. Your consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of doing business with us or scheduling a tour.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. We will only text you when it is relevant to your inquiry or event. We are not a high-volume marketing operation — you will not receive spam, promotions, or automated broadcast messages.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Chatsworth Plantation does not charge for SMS messages, but your carrier's standard rates may apply.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any text message you receive from us. You may also text CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, or QUIT — we will honor any reasonable opt-out request. After you opt out, we will send one final confirmation message and then cease all SMS contact. You may also contact us directly at info@chatsworthplantation.com to request removal.

We will honor all opt-out requests within 10 business days in accordance with FCC regulations effective April 11, 2025.

How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any text message for assistance, or contact us directly at info@chatsworthplantation.com or (225) 263-2202.

Supported Carriers

SMS messaging is available through most major U.S. carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others. Carrier support may vary.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Chatsworth Plantation does not sell, rent, or share your personal information — including your mobile phone number — with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes. Your contact information is used only for communication directly related to your inquiry with us.

We may use trusted service providers (such as email or SMS platforms) to help us communicate with you. These providers are contractually prohibited from using your information for any purpose other than facilitating our communications.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information you share with us. Your contact details are stored securely and accessed only by members of our team who need them to respond to your inquiry.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to serve you and fulfill any obligations related to your event. If you have opted in to SMS and later opt out, your phone number will be removed from our active contact list promptly. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at info@chatsworthplantation.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

  • Email: info@chatsworthplantation.com
  • Phone: (225) 263-2202
  • Address: 5755 LA-74, Saint Gabriel, LA 70776
