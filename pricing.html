Catering & Bar

Southern Cuisine,
Made In-House

Connie and the Chatsworth kitchen take great pride in the food they serve. From southern comfort dishes to elevated Cajun and Creole flavors, every menu is crafted in-house with care. No outside caterers or outside alcohol are permitted — and once you taste the food, you will understand why. Bar packages range from soft drinks through full top-shelf bar service. Reach out for our complete catering and bar menu.

All events include a 9.45% Iberville Parish sales tax on all fees.
